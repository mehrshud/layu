Our Course Levels

We offer courses at every level to ensure your language learning journey is continuous and comprehensive.

A1 Beginner

Start your language journey with basic vocabulary, simple phrases, and fundamental grammar. Ideal for absolute beginners with no prior knowledge.

A2 Elementary

Build on basic knowledge to form simple sentences, engage in basic conversations, and understand everyday expressions.

B1 Intermediate

Develop more complex language skills, express opinions, and understand the main points of clear texts on familiar topics.

B2 Upper Intermediate

Express yourself fluently on a wide range of topics, understand complex texts, and interact with native speakers with ease.

C1 Advanced

Achieve near-native proficiency, understand demanding texts, and express ideas fluently without searching for expressions.

About Our Academy

Founded in 2005, the Language Academy of Yazd University has been a center of excellence in language education.

Excellence in Language Education

The Language Academy of Yazd University is dedicated to providing high-quality language education to students, professionals, and language enthusiasts. Our academy combines traditional teaching methods with modern technology to create an engaging and effective learning environment.

With over 15 years of experience, we have helped thousands of students achieve their language goals, whether for academic purposes, career advancement, or personal growth.

Our team of qualified instructors, many of whom are native speakers, are passionate about teaching and committed to your success. We offer small class sizes to ensure personalized attention and maximum speaking practice.
